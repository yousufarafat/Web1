Company Overview

Milky Way It Solution is a Freelance-based IT Agency and Training institute in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Our main vision is to provide high-quality services to satisfy clients worldwide. Also, we have another vision to develop qualified freelancers in Bangladesh who can generate revenue for our country and contribute to the society.

We want to share our acquired knowledge with the community. Also, we want to provide quality educational opportunities to develop and enable trainees to realize their potential by strengthening their knowledge, IT skills, and Educational values.

  • Project Based IT Training
  • Training for Backward Communities

  • Professional Freelancing Course
  • IT Consultancy For Freelancing Agency

About CEO

Mmrs Mamun is the CEO of this organization. He is a dreamer who has almost 10+ years of experience in the IT industry. Also, he has more than 7+ years of corporate job experience related to freelancing. He worked with a freelancing-based reputed company under the agency profile.

On May 1, 2022, he quit his job and started working on the most popular marketplace Like Fiverr, Upwork, Freelancer, and PPH to build his freelance career under his own profile. During his corporate career, he has worked with a few thousand international clients over the world under his X-agency with various types of services.

Our CEO believes that a peaceful environment is greater than money. Now, his dream is to create a job place where all of the employees learn & earn with self respect.
